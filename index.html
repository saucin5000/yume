Akinosuke no Yume (YUME) is a new cryptocurrency that is set to fulfill the decentralization dreams of Ryoshi, the creator of Shiba Inu. YUME is designed to be a fully decentralized ecosystem that will empower individuals and promote financial freedom.

Ryoshi, the creator of Shiba Inu, is a pioneer of decentralization in the cryptocurrency world. His beliefs in decentralization have been the foundation of all his projects, including Shiba Inu. However, Ryoshi realized that Shiba Inu had limitations that prevented him from fully realizing his vision of decentralization.
